Pull-Back Machine Component – Tubes Investment of India (TI Machine Building)

As part of my engineering internship at Tubes Investment of India, I was involved in the design and manufacturing of a critical component for a pipe production machine, specifically a section known as the Pull-Back Machine.

This component plays a key role in the exit phase of the pipe manufacturing process. After a pipe completes its shaping and processing, the pull-back mechanism ensures it is safely guided out of the production line without deformation, misalignment, or surface damage.

Machine Integration

  • Precision machined guide elements that manage pipe handling after forming
  • Structural supports designed for durability under repetitive operation
  • Surfaces engineered to reduce friction and wear during pipe movement

My Contribution

  • Assisting with part development and assembly planning
  • Understanding the flow of operations at the machine’s exit stage
  • Gaining exposure to heavy-duty industrial fixtures and pipe-handling systems

This experience significantly deepened my understanding of mechanical handling systems in production lines and gave me practical insight into automation and machine design workflows used in the heavy manufacturing industry.
